No technical background is required, but you must be detail-oriented and ready to learn technical skills (like setting up CRM, email systems, and funnels). This is a role for motivated individuals who can execute.

While results vary based on effort, students typically start outreach by week 3. Many close their first low-to-mid-tier retainer within the first 6-8 weeks, with high-ticket partnerships following shortly after.

What makes a $10k/month career sustainable?

Sustainability comes from value-based pricing (rev-share models) tied to essential operations (not easily replaced) and partnering with high-growth creators who desperately need systems to scale, not just simple task execution.
